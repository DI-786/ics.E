Effects on Plants

Negative Effects

Tourism impacts plants and flowers through direct physical damage—trampling, illegal picking, and vehicle use—and indirect effects, including the introduction of invasive species, pathogen spread (e.g., Phytophthora cinnamomi), and soil compaction, which reduces species diversity. Conversely, sustainable tourism can support habitat restoration.

  1. Trampling and Physical damage
  2. Soil Compaction and corrosion
  3. Introductions of Pathogens and weeds
  4. Destructuion for Infrastructure
  5. Illegal harvesting

Positive Effects

  1. Habitat Restoration and Funding
  2. Agrotourism and Botanical Education
  3. Conservation and Management
